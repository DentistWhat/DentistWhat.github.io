The Ultimate Shelter: A Plugin

This plugin is in development by DentistWhat, and is his first Bukkit plugin. Naturally it's incredibly flawed, but he's trying, and that's what counts.

The Basic Idea

The Ultimate Shelter Challenge is a brilliant idea, and the Dentist loved it so much he decided to go ahead and attempt to create a plugin listing the rules of the Challenge, as well as keep track of current status of the challenge.

Will this plugin ever be complete?

Probably not, no. The Dentist is far too lazy.
